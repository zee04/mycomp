The new age of amazing plant based alternative proteins

March 15, 2024 Innovation

Plant based alternative proteins are driving the next food revolution. These alternatives have unique potential as they are innovative while being a sustainable source of protein without animals being subjected to cruelty. While the entire world is shifting gears towards sustainability, plant-based proteins are becoming mainstream.

The market for plant-based meat alternatives is experiencing exponential growth, with consumers increasingly seeking healthier and more sustainable food options. Our consultancy specializes in helping cloud kitchens integrate these innovative protein sources into their menu offerings.

From jackfruit-based pulled "pork" to pea protein burgers, we help restaurants create compelling plant-based alternatives that satisfy even the most dedicated meat lovers. The key is understanding flavor profiles, textures, and cooking techniques that make these alternatives truly appealing.

Converting Non vegetarians

February 28, 2024 Strategy

My journey in the food industry started at the Central kitchen of Taj Palace and Hotels. Everything was new to me. My wide eyes used to follow every step of chef and commies. Staff food was nutritious and interesting to me. It continued later when I started to work in various kitchens across the country.

The challenge of converting non-vegetarians to plant-based options requires a deep understanding of taste preferences, cultural backgrounds, and psychological factors that influence food choices. Through years of experience, we've developed proven strategies that work.

The secret lies in not trying to replicate meat exactly, but in creating dishes that are so delicious and satisfying that customers don't miss meat at all. It's about showcasing the incredible flavors and textures that plant-based ingredients can offer when prepared with skill and creativity.

Advent of Cloud Kitchens

February 10, 2024 Industry

Kitchens that have no dining facility, no seats, no servers are called ghost/dark/cloud kitchens. Orders are received through an App or the customer calls the kitchen directly/internet-based ordering. Cloud Kitchens - the future Such kitchens are the future of the restaurant industry.

The COVID-19 pandemic accelerated the adoption of cloud kitchens worldwide, but this trend was already gaining momentum due to changing consumer behaviors and the rise of food delivery platforms. Cloud kitchens offer numerous advantages including lower overhead costs, faster setup times, and greater flexibility in menu experimentation.

However, success in the cloud kitchen space requires specialized knowledge in areas such as delivery logistics, digital marketing, kitchen efficiency optimization, and maintaining food quality during transport. Our consultancy addresses all these critical factors to ensure sustainable success.
